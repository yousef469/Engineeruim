### Bridge Module 1: Rockets & Aerospace (6 lessons)
**Connects**: Calculus, DEs, vectors, forces, energy, thermodynamics

1. **Rocket Equation Derivation** - Tsiolkovsky equation from momentum conservation
2. **Trajectory Optimization** - Calculus of variations, optimal paths

4. **Thrust Vector Control** - Vector math, rotation matrices, gimbal angles
5. **Aerodynamic Forces** - Drag equation, lift coefficient, Reynolds number
6. **Propulsion Thermodynamics** - Isp, nozzle expansion, combustion

### Bridge Module 2: Aircraft & Aeronautics (6 lessons)
**Connects**: Vectors, forces, fluids, Bernoulli, stability, control

1. **Lift & Drag Analysis** - Airfoil math, angle of attack, stall
2. **Flight Dynamics** - 6-DOF equations, Euler angles, quaternions
3. **Stability & Control** - Transfer functions, pole placement, autopilot
4. **Propeller/Jet Thrust** - Momentum theory, Bernoulli application
5. **Structural Loads** - Bending moments, shear forces, wing loading
6. **Navigation Math** - Great circles, wind triangles, INS

### Bridge Module 3: Automotive Engineering (6 lessons)
**Connects**: Forces, energy, rotational motion, friction, circuits

1. **Vehicle Dynamics** - Tire forces, weight transfer, handling
2. **Powertrain Analysis** - Torque curves, gear ratios, transmission
3. **Suspension Design** - Spring-mass-damper, ride comfort, handling
4. **Braking Systems** - Friction, heat dissipation, ABS
5. **Electric Vehicle Math** - Battery capacity, motor torque, range
6. **Aerodynamics & Efficiency** - Drag reduction, fuel economy

### Bridge Module 4: Electronics & Circuits (6 lessons)
**Connects**: Complex numbers, DEs, Laplace, E&M, signals

1. **AC Circuit Analysis** - Phasors, impedance, power factor
2. **Filter Design** - Transfer functions, Bode plots, frequency response
3. **Transistor Circuits** - Amplifiers, biasing, small-signal analysis
4. **Digital Logic** - Boolean algebra, gates, timing
5. **Microcontroller Math** - ADC/DAC, PWM, sampling theory
6. **Communication Systems** - Modulation, Fourier analysis, bandwidth
