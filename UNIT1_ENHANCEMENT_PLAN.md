# Unit 1 Enhancement Plan - Algebra & Equations

## Goal
Enhance all 6 lessons in Unit 1 to match the quality and engagement level of Units 2-5 (which are already enhanced).

## Enhancement Strategy

### What Makes Enhanced Lessons Great:
1. **25-minute duration** (vs current 15-20 min)
2. **Visual ASCII diagrams** to illustrate concepts
3. **Step-by-step breakdowns** with clear examples
4. **Real engineering scenarios** that students can relate to
5. **Interactive challenges** that feel like games
6. **Engaging tone** - professional but fun, not boring
7. **Multiple practice problems** with full solutions
8. **"Your Turn" sections** for active learning

### Lessons to Enhance:

**Lesson 1: Variables, Units & Symbols** ⏱️ 15 min → 25 min
- Add visual unit conversion diagrams
- NASA disaster story with more detail
- Interactive unit conversion challenges
- Robot/drone examples throughout

**Lesson 2: Linear Equations** ⏱️ 15 min → 25 min
- Visual balance scale diagrams
- Step-by-step equation solving with graphics
- Real engineering problems (circuits, motion)
- Challenge: Design a system using equations

**Lesson 3: Quadratic Equations** ⏱️ 20 min → 25 min
- Parabola visualizations
- Projectile motion graphics
- Rocket trajectory examples
- Interactive: Design a jump/launch

**Lesson 4: Systems of Equations** ⏱️ 20 min → 25 min
- Visual representation of 2-3 equations
- Force balance diagrams
- Circuit analysis examples
- Multi-drone coordination challenge

**Lesson 5: Exponents & Powers** ⏱️ 18 min → 25 min
- Exponential growth visualizations
- Compound interest examples
- Battery decay scenarios
- Moore's Law and technology growth

**Lesson 6: Logarithms** ⏱️ 20 min → 25 min
- Decibel scale visualizations
- pH scale examples
- Sensor calibration scenarios
- Earthquake magnitude comparisons
